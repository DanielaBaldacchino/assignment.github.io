I am Daniela Baldacchino, a 20 year ol Maltese based artist. I am currently following a Digital Art course. I am a multi-media aritist, where my work varies from highly detailed artworks to very minimalistic. I initially started working on detailed traditional artworks, however, I have grown a passion for simple digital illustrations. Art is my means of expressing my deepest thoughts and feelings, I hope to inspire other people through my art.
